Virtual Reality hasn't even hit the mainstream yet and already there are millions of Google Cardboards and other compatible mobile phone powered head mounted displays in the hands and on the faces of consumers. The availability of these devices make them a very attractive platform to develop games and experiences for, but their simplicity and immaturity make targeting them a challenge. In this free mini-course I'm going to take you through the basics of using Unity to develop games and experiences for the Google Cardboard and related mobile phone VR devices.
